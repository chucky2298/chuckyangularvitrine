Harga

A new TV series called ‘Harga’, by director Lassaad Oueslati, has brought to Tunisian living rooms during the month of Ramadan the plight of illegal migrants as they struggle to flee poverty at home and battle to survive the treacherous journey across the sea.

Written by Imed Eddine Hakim, Harga tells the story of a group of Tunisians from different parts of the country who don’t know each other and find themselves crossing the Mediterranean to Italy aboard a rickety boat.
